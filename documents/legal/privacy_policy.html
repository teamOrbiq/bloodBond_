Privacy Policy

BloodBond respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our application.

Information We Collect

We may collect personal information including your full name, email address, phone number, blood group, profile photo, age range, gender (optional), and location information. Emergency contact details may also be collected if provided by the user. Some data is optional and can be controlled by the user.

When using Google Sign-In, we may receive basic profile information such as name, email address, and profile photo for authentication purposes.

We may also collect device and usage information such as app version, device type, crash logs, and analytics data to improve performance.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to create and manage accounts, connect blood donors with recipients, enable communication between users, improve app functionality, ensure platform safety, and send important notifications.

We do not sell or share personal data with advertisers or third parties for marketing purposes.

Data Sharing

Some profile information such as name, blood group, availability status, location, and contact details may be visible to other users when required for blood donation purposes.

We only share data when necessary for app functionality, legal requirements, or security purposes with trusted service providers.

Location Usage

Location data is used to show nearby donors and requests. Users can disable location access anytime from device settings, but some features may not work properly.

Chatbot & Medical Disclaimer

Any AI or chatbot responses are for informational purposes only and do not replace professional medical advice. Always consult qualified healthcare professionals in case of emergencies.

Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational security measures to protect your data. However, no system is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

User Control

Users can update profile information, manage visibility, block or report users, disable permissions, and request account deletion at any time.

Account deletion can be requested directly from the app or by contacting support.

Data Retention

We retain data only as long as necessary to provide services, comply with legal obligations, and maintain platform safety. Data is deleted securely when no longer needed.

Children’s Privacy

BloodBond is intended for users aged 18+. Users under 18 should use the app under parental supervision. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Third-Party Services

We may use trusted third-party services such as authentication, analytics, and cloud providers. These services follow their own privacy policies.

Permissions

The app may request access to location, camera, photos, notifications, and storage only for essential functionality. Users can control permissions anytime.

Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Continued use of the app means acceptance of any changes.

Final Legal Note

BloodBond is a technology platform only. We do not collect, store, or distribute blood and do not provide medical services or emergency healthcare.
