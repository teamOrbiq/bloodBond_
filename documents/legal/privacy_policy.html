Privacy Policy

BloodBond values your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, share, and protect the data you provide when using our application.

Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide and improve our services. This may include basic details such as your name, email, phone number, blood group, location, and optional profile information. We may also collect app usage data to ensure the platform operates efficiently and safely.

How We Use Your Information

Your data is used primarily to verify your identity, connect donors with those in need of blood, and enable secure communication within the app. Additionally, we use this information to improve app functionality and enhance user safety. We do not sell or share personal data with advertisers for marketing purposes.

Data Sharing

Your phone number and personal details remain private and are only revealed to other users when necessary for facilitating a blood donation. We share data solely to maintain app functionality and do not disclose information to third-party advertisers or unrelated entities.

Chatbot & Medical Disclaimer

Any advice provided by the AI chatbot is general in nature and does not replace professional medical guidance. For serious health conditions or decisions, please consult a qualified healthcare provider.

Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information from unauthorized access or disclosure. However, no digital system can be completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute protection.

User Control

You have control over your account and information. Users can update profile details, change availability status, report or block other users, and request account deletion at any time.

Children's Privacy

BloodBond is intended for individuals aged 18 and above. Users under 18 should access the platform only with parental or guardian supervision.

Data Retention

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations. Once data is no longer needed, it is securely deleted.

Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Continued use of the app signifies acceptance of any changes or revisions.
