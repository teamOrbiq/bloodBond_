BloodBond Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 2026

BloodBond is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal data is handled in a safe, transparent, and responsible manner. This Privacy Policy explains in detail how we collect, use, store, share, and protect your information when you use the BloodBond mobile application and related services. By accessing or using BloodBond, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

To operate a reliable blood donation platform, we collect certain personal and technical information from users. This may include your full name, email address, phone number, blood group, profile details, and approximate location. These details are necessary to connect blood donors and recipients efficiently and safely.

Users may optionally provide additional information such as profile photos, gender, emergency contact details, and availability status. This optional data helps improve trust and communication within the platform.

When you sign in using Google authentication, we may receive limited information such as your name, email address, and profile picture strictly for authentication and account management purposes.

We also collect technical information such as device type, operating system version, app version, crash logs, analytics data, and usage behavior. This information is used to improve app performance, stability, security, and user experience.

Location data may be collected to help users find nearby donors and blood requests. Users can disable location access at any time, although certain features may not function properly without it.

2. How We Use Information

We use collected information strictly to operate, maintain, and improve the BloodBond platform. This includes creating and managing user accounts, enabling donor-recipient matching, displaying relevant blood requests, and facilitating communication between users.

Information is also used to enhance platform performance, improve safety systems, provide customer support, send important notifications, and prevent fraud, abuse, or unauthorized activity.

We do not sell, rent, or trade personal data to advertisers or third-party marketing companies under any circumstances.

3. Data Sharing & User Visibility

BloodBond is a community-based platform where certain user information such as name, blood group, availability status, and approximate location may be visible to other users when necessary for blood donation coordination.

We only share data with trusted third-party service providers who assist in operating the platform such as hosting, analytics, or authentication services. These providers are bound by strict confidentiality and data protection obligations.

We may also disclose information if required by law, legal process, or to protect the safety, rights, or security of users and the platform.
