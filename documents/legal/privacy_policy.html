BloodBond Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 2026

BloodBond respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal information of every user. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, share, and safeguard information when you use the BloodBond mobile application and related services.

By accessing or using BloodBond, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

To provide a reliable and efficient blood donation platform, BloodBond may collect certain personal and technical information from users. This may include your full name, email address, phone number, blood group, profile details, approximate location information, and other information necessary to connect donors and recipients effectively. Certain profile information, such as profile photos, gender, or emergency contact details, may be optional and provided voluntarily by users.

When users sign in using Google Sign-In, we may receive limited account information from Google, including your name, email address, and profile photo strictly for authentication and account management purposes.

We may also collect limited technical and usage-related information such as device type, operating system version, app version, crash reports, analytics data, and feature usage statistics to improve app performance, reliability, and security.

Location information may be used to help users discover nearby blood donors and blood requests more efficiently. Users can manage or disable location permissions through their device settings at any time, although certain features may not function properly without location access.

2. How We Use Information

BloodBond uses collected information to operate, maintain, and improve the platform. Information may be used to create and manage user accounts, connect donors with recipients, display relevant blood requests, enable communication between users, improve platform performance, provide customer support, send important service notifications, prevent abuse or fraudulent activity, and maintain overall platform safety and security.

We do not sell, rent, or trade personal information to advertisers or unrelated third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Sharing & User Visibility

BloodBond functions as a community-driven platform that helps facilitate communication between blood donors and individuals requesting blood donations. In order to enable these services, certain user information such as name, blood group, donor availability status, approximate location, and contact information may become visible to other users when necessary for donation coordination.

Personal information is not shared with unrelated third parties except where required for legal compliance, platform security, fraud prevention, or essential operational services provided by trusted service providers working on behalf of BloodBond under appropriate confidentiality obligations.

4. AI Assistance & Medical Disclaimer

BloodBond may provide AI-powered assistance or informational chatbot features for general informational purposes only. Any information, suggestions, or responses generated through these features should not be considered professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.

Users should always consult qualified healthcare professionals for medical decisions, emergencies, or health-related concerns. BloodBond does not provide healthcare services, emergency medical support, or professional medical consultation.

5. Data Security

BloodBond implements reasonable technical, administrative, and organizational security measures designed to protect user information from unauthorized access, misuse, alteration, disclosure, or loss. While we strive to maintain strong security standards, no digital platform or internet-based service can guarantee complete security, and users acknowledge that they share information at their own discretion and risk.

6. User Rights & Account Controls

Users maintain control over their personal information and may update profile details, manage donor availability status, control visibility preferences, report or block users, and manage app permissions directly through the application or device settings.

Users may also request permanent deletion of their account and associated data either directly within the app or by contacting support. Certain limited information may be temporarily retained where necessary to comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, enforce policies, or prevent fraudulent activity.

7. Data Retention

BloodBond retains user information only for as long as reasonably necessary to provide services, maintain platform functionality, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce applicable policies. When information is no longer required, it is securely deleted, anonymized, or disposed of using reasonable security practices.

8. Children's Privacy

BloodBond is intended primarily for individuals aged 18 years or older. Users under the age of 18 should access or use the platform only under the supervision of a parent or legal guardian. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children without appropriate authorization or consent.

9. Third-Party Services

BloodBond may rely on trusted third-party services and infrastructure providers, including authentication providers, cloud hosting services, analytics tools, notification systems, and database services necessary for platform functionality and reliability. These third-party providers may process limited information in accordance with their own privacy and security policies.

Depending on device functionality and app features, BloodBond may request access to certain permissions including location, notifications, camera, photos, media, storage, or internet access solely to support intended platform features and user experience. Users can manage or revoke permissions at any time through device settings.
